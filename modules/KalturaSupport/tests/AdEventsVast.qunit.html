VAST 3.0 Ad Events

This test page demonstrates ad events flow. Using an ad blocker software will also trigger ad error events.

The expected events:

For pre-roll ads:
  • preSequenceStart: is triggered by the Vidiun player to mark the beginning of the pre-sequence event
  • AdSupport_StartAdPlayback: is triggered by the Vidiun player when the first pre-sequence ad starts
  • onAdPlay and onAdComplete events are triggered by the VAST plugin for each ad in the sequence *
  • AdSupport_EndAdPlayback: is triggered by the Vidiun player when the last pre-sequence ad finish
  • preSequenceComplete: is triggered by the Vidiun player to mark the end of the pre-sequence event
For mid-roll ads:
  • midSequenceStart: is triggered by the Vidiun player to mark the beginning of the mid-sequence event
  • AdSupport_StartAdPlayback: is triggered by the Vidiun player when the first mid-sequence ad starts
  • onAdPlay and onAdComplete events are triggered by the VAST plugin for each ad in the sequence *
  • AdSupport_EndAdPlayback: is triggered by the Vidiun player when the last mid-sequence ad finish
  • midSequenceComplete: is triggered by the Vidiun player to mark the end of the mid-sequence event
For overlay ads:
  • onAdPlay and onAdComplete events are triggered by the VAST plugin for each overlay ad
For post-roll ads:
  • postSequenceStart: is triggered by the Vidiun player to mark the beginning of the post-sequence event
  • AdSupport_StartAdPlayback: is triggered by the Vidiun player when the first post-sequence ad starts
  • onAdPlay and onAdComplete events are triggered by the VAST plugin for each ad in the sequence *
  • AdSupport_EndAdPlayback: is triggered by the Vidiun player when the last post-sequence ad finish
  • postSequenceComplete: is triggered by the Vidiun player to mark the end of the post-sequence event
Ad skip event:
  • onAdSkip: is triggered when the ad is skipped (when the user clicks the "Skip" button)
Ad click event:
  • adClick: is triggered when the ad is clicked by the user
Ad error events:
  • adErrorEvent: is triggered when the ad fails to load (usually caused by ad blocker usage)

* Note: onAdPlay and onAdComplete events are triggered per ad and are not part of the player event flow.
